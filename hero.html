Safety Tips

Stay Aware, Stay Prepared

  • • Stay in crowded place and look for campgrounds with security measures (e.g., gates, entry passes, security guards)
  • • Making friends at the campsite helps with security and looking out for each other
  • • Use fake signals to appear less vulnerable, like:
  • - Leaving out large men's shoes outside the van
  • - Placing a fake person (doll/mannequin) in the camper to deter potential threats.
  • - Keeping tools or heavy gear visible to suggest someone strong is nearby. Avoid weapons as it may backfire if someone breaks in and finds them before you.
  • • Find better lit secure overnight spots for camping and do research on popular areas with security. If you still feel unsure check if there are women-only campsites in your area. Always check reviews.
  • • Make sure the campsite is not too far from help service.
  • • If you are going camping in the wild, people are not your only concern, research the wild life around.
  • • When going further away from your campervan, make sure to make it look like you do not have anything valuable and hide well expensive things.
  • • Carry pepper spray, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit.
  • • Carry extra locks for extra security.
