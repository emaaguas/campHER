Safety Tips

Stay Aware, Stay Prepared

    Safety Tip

    Stay in crowded places and look for campgrounds with security measures (e.g., gates, entry passes, security guards).

    Build trust

    Making friends at the campsite helps with security and looking out for each other.

    Smart Disguises

    Use fake signals to appear less vulnerable, like: Leaving out large men's shoes outside the van and keeping tools or heavy gear visible to suggest someone strong is nearby.

    Sleep Safe

    Find better lit secure overnight spots for camping and do research on popular areas with security. If you still feel unsure check if there are women-only campsites in your area. Always check reviews.

    Stay Nearby

    Make sure the campsite is not too far from help service.

    Stay Allert

    Carry pepper spray, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit.
