About Us

Hi, I’m Camp.her, your go-to vanlife buddy built for solo female travelers.

I’m here to make exploring feel exciting, not stressful. No more texting five people “just in case something happens.” With me, you’ve got safe places to stay, helpful tips, and a community that actually gets what it’s like to travel solo as a woman.

Whether you’re heading out for the weekend or planning a bigger escape, I’ll be right there, giving you freedom, peace of mind, and a comfy place to call home on the road.

Our Vision

We imagine a world where women can hit the road without looking over their shoulder. Where solo travel feels less like a risk and more like a right. Where every turn leads to freedom, not fear.

We’re here to make road travel feel safer, easier, and more inspiring for women, by creating a space where support, simplicity, and community come together. Because adventure should feel exciting, not like something you have to overthink.
